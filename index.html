Dr. Hendranus Vermeulen

Mad Scientist

I am passionate about education and technology. I am interested in the dialectical process which unites human cognition and the development of tools. I have dedicated the last 15 years towards developing my skills, experience and understanding of learning, collaboration and digital innovation through various multidisciplinary research and development projects in industry and academia. I am seeking opportunities to collaborate with others that share this passion and seek to apply our experience creatively to make a positive change in the world.
